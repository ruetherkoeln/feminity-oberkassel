Welcome to Feminity by Jule

A bilingual beauty space in the heart of Düsseldorf-Oberkassel — for hair, skin, and personal care. We welcome English-speaking clients from the local expat communities (Japanese, American, British and beyond) as well as international visitors staying at the Me&All Hotel.

Julrine "Jule" Domingos is a Master Beautician with experience on television productions, fashion shoots, and runway shows. Master Hair Stylist Nicky Rouge specializes in balayage, color expertise, English cuts, and curly hair.

Our Services

Signature treatments — explained in plain English.

Hair

Precision cuts, color work, balayage, curl styling and updos. Nicky Rouge brings English-cut technique and is a recognized curl specialist. From €89.

Skin & Facials

Green Peel® herbal peeling for radiant skin, Neovita treatments suitable for sensitive and allergy-prone skin, customized facials by Master Beautician Jule. From €215.

Permanent Makeup (PMU)

Powder brows and lip blush by certified specialists. Natural, precise results — ideal for clients who want to simplify their daily routine. Price on request.

Brow Lamination

The non-invasive alternative to PMU. Lasts 6–8 weeks, perfect for travel-heavy lifestyles. Ask about our brow consultation.

Nd:YAG Laser Hair Removal

Safe for all skin types — including darker complexions where other lasers fall short. Honest expectations, no overselling.

Beauty Drips & NAD+

Vitamin and mineral infusions for skin radiance and energy. NAD+ drips for cellular anti-aging. From €150.

FAQ

Common questions from our English-speaking clients.

Is Feminity by Jule an English-speaking hair salon in Düsseldorf?

Yes. Our team speaks English and German. We regularly welcome international clients living in Düsseldorf-Oberkassel and nearby expat communities — Japanese, American, British and other English speakers feel at home here.

Where is Feminity by Jule located?

Inside the Me&All Hotel, Hansaallee 1a, 40549 Düsseldorf-Oberkassel. The salon is accessible via the in-house underground parking with a direct elevator.

What are your opening hours?

Monday and Tuesday 10:00–19:00, Wednesday to Friday 10:00–22:00, Saturday 10:00–18:00. Closed on Sunday. The Wed–Fri late hours are ideal if you work during the day.

What services do you offer?

Hair (cut, color, balayage, curls, updos), facial and skin treatments (Green Peel®, Neovita), permanent makeup (powder brows, lip blush), Nd:YAG laser hair removal, brow lamination, and beauty drips including NAD+ infusions.

How do I book an appointment?

Book online via our Treatwell page (English supported), email us at 40549@feminity-oberkassel.de, or call +49 176 87438736 during opening hours.

Ready to book?

Pick the time that suits you — we are open Mon and Tue 10am until 7pm, Wed–Fri until 10pm, Sat from 10am to 6pm.
